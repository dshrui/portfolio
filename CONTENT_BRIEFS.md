# Portfolio Illustration Briefs

These briefs guided the redesigned portfolio posters in `index.html`.

## Brand Reference: adough

- Source reviewed: `https://www.instagram.com/a_dough__/`
- Business category: artisan sourdough bakery with locations in Sri Hartamas and Bukit Bintang.
- Visible palette: warm cream/beige, deep navy, Mediterranean blue, muted green storefront/awning, pastry brown, and clean white signage.
- Visual cues: editorial bakery photography, storefront lifestyle shots, close-up food textures, soft natural light, and a quiet premium cafe mood.
- Application rule: use the palette and visual mood as reference only; do not copy Instagram images directly.

## 1. Google Review Poster

- Client: adough, artisan sourdough bakery.
- Objective: encourage customers to leave a Google review immediately after dine-in or takeaway.
- Audience: bakery customers who enjoyed bread, pastries, coffee, or the store experience.
- Core message: "Loved your bake?" is warmer and more context-specific than a plain "Google Review" label.
- Required content: brand mark, review prompt, fictitious QR code, short instruction flow, and small note that the QR is illustrative.
- Design direction: cream/navy/blue bakery palette, realistic sourdough and coffee background, table-card composition, editorial serif headline, and clear review action flow.
- Background prompt used: photorealistic editorial food photography for artisan sourdough bakery social media poster, rustic sourdough loaf sliced open on cream bakery counter, flaky pastry crumbs, ceramic coffee cup, warm morning window light, deep navy cloth napkin, Mediterranean blue accent plate, muted green storefront reflection, premium Malaysian bakery atmosphere, large clean negative space in top left for typography, no text, no logo, no watermark, 4:5 portrait.

## 2. April Self-Care Menu

- Client: GlowHaus Beauty, beauty and self-care service business.
- Objective: turn a service list into a seasonal booking poster for April.
- Audience: customers browsing Instagram or WhatsApp who want a quick facial, lash, scalp, or glow package.
- Core message: "Reset your glow ritual." positions the menu around a seasonal self-care reason, not just a price list.
- Required content: campaign title, short booking context, four service items with prices, and booking urgency by date.
- Design direction: soft rose/plum beauty palette, realistic spa product background, editorial serif headline, structured service rows, and a quiet booking CTA.
- Background prompt retained as fallback: photorealistic premium beauty salon self-care menu background, rose clay and warm ivory palette, facial serum bottles, folded towel, gua sha stone, facial brush, soft flowers, spa counter surface, natural window light, elegant commercial editorial realism, clean negative space for typography and service prices, no text, no logo, no watermark, 4:5 portrait.

## 3. Wedding Invitation

- Client: fictional Malay wedding client, Aisyah and Daniel.
- Objective: create an elegant WhatsApp-ready wedding e-invite with RSVP, map, and itinerary sections.
- Audience: wedding guests receiving the invite digitally.
- Core message: a calm invitation-first composition where names, date, venue, and time are instantly clear.
- Required content: event label, couple monogram, couple names, invitation line, date, venue, time, and RSVP/map/itinerary add-ons.
- Design direction: champagne silk, white florals, warm window light, translucent invitation surface, centered content panel, refined serif typography, and understated wine accent.
- Background prompt used: photorealistic elegant Malay wedding invitation background, champagne silk fabric, cream jasmine flowers, soft white orchids, gold wedding rings, translucent vellum invitation card surface without writing, warm window light, refined editorial composition, clear centered negative space for couple names and event details, premium understated, no text, no logo, no watermark, 4:5 portrait.
